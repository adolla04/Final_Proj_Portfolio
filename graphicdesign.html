GRAPHIC DESIGN

Flowers and Fairies Color Theory Study

Fairies and Flowers is a photo collage of various images I altered using a combination of adjustment layers, cropping, threshold, and color tools to create a new design. Next, I conducted a color study, practicing various color relationships and techniques like temperature, extension, complementary and value to create color combinations that will catch the viewer's eye.
